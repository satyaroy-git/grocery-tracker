# PantryPal - Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026

## Overview

PantryPal ("we", "our", "the app") is a grocery inventory management app. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

### Data stored locally (on your device only):
- Grocery items (names, quantities, categories)
- Shopping lists
- Consumption logs
- Price history
- App settings and preferences

**This data never leaves your device** unless you use the Family Sharing feature.

### Family Sharing (optional):
If you choose to use Family Sharing:
- Your display name (what you enter)
- A random device identifier
- Shared shopping list items
- Shared pantry data

This data is stored on our cloud servers (Supabase) and is only accessible to members of your family group.

### Data we do NOT collect:
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Passwords
- Location data
- Contacts
- Photos (barcode scans are processed locally)
- Browsing history
- Advertising identifiers

## Third-Party Services

- **Supabase** (Family Sharing sync): [supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy)
- **Open Food Facts** (Barcode lookup): [openfoodfacts.org/privacy](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/privacy)
- **TheMealDB** (Recipe suggestions): Public free API, no user data sent

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties.

## Data Deletion

- **Local data:** Uninstall the app to delete all local data, or use Settings → Reset All Data
- **Family Sharing data:** Leave the family group to disassociate your data, or contact us for deletion

## Children's Privacy

PantryPal does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date.
