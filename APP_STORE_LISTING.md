## Full Description

Never run out of essentials again! PantryPal is your smart kitchen companion that helps you manage groceries, track consumption, and shop smarter.

KEY FEATURES:

PANTRY MANAGEMENT
- Add items with quantity, category, and low-stock threshold
- Color-coded status: green (stocked), yellow (low), red (out)
- Set expiry dates and get alerts before food goes bad
- Multi-select to delete items in bulk

SMART TRACKING
- Manual logging: quickly log how much you used
- Auto-estimate: set a consumption rate, app deducts automatically
- Price tracking: see how much you spend on groceries monthly

SHOPPING MADE EASY
- Auto-generate shopping lists from low-stock items
- Save recurring lists as templates (Weekly Basics, Monthly Staples)
- Share directly to WhatsApp with one tap
- Mark items purchased and update stock automatically

BARCODE SCANNER
- Scan any product barcode to instantly add to pantry
- Looks up product name, brand, and category automatically

RECIPE SUGGESTIONS
- Get recipe ideas based on what's in your pantry
- 12+ built-in Indian recipes (Dal Tadka, Paneer Butter Masala, Chole & more)
- Full step-by-step cooking instructions

FAMILY SHARING
- Create a family group with a simple 6-digit code
- Share shopping lists in real-time with family members
- Sync pantry data across devices — no login required!

QUICK IMPORT
- Paste order text from Blinkit, Instamart, Flipkart, Zepto
- Auto-detects items, quantities, and categories
- Review and import to pantry in seconds

WHY PANTRYPAL?
- Works 100% offline (no internet needed for core features)
- No account or login required
- Clean, fast interface designed for daily use
- Supports Indian grocery items and brands
- Free to use!

Download PantryPal today and take control of your kitchen!
